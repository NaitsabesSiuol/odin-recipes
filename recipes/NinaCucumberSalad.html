Blueberry Pie

The recipe for this easy salad came from my grandma,

who made it in the summers with fresh dill and cucumbers from her garden.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup reduced-fat sour cream (such as DaisyÂ®)
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice/li>
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill/li>
  • 3 cucumbers, halved lengthwise and thinly sliced crosswise/li>
  • 1/2 cup thinly sliced red onion
  • coarse salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Mix sour cream, lemon juice, and dill together in a bowl.
  2. Add cucumbers and onion,
  3. Stir to combine,
  4. Season with salt and black pepper.
